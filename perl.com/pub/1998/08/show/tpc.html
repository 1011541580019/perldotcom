Well, it has been almost a year and it is time again for The Perl Conference! I was all set to go last year, and a great job opportunity came up that I couldn't refuse - but then my (soon to be ex) employer refused to send me.

My name is Brent Michalski and I have the honor of being the "roaming reporter" for O'Reilly at this year's conference. I will be covering the key issues of the conference and bringing you daily updates on conference happenings.

This years conference promises to be full of pizza and beer with a little Perl thrown in. People had a great time and brought back a lot of knowledge last year - this year the conference promises to be bigger and better. If you are like me, you are getting excited about the conference too. Yes, I know I'm a computer geek - but who are the ones making the money AND loving their jobs??? It sure isn't the "jocks" we knew in school!

If you spot me, please stop by and say hello! I'll be the guy in the Perl shirt :-) Actually, I don't know what I will be wearing, but I am not that hard to spot. I am the guy with the Iron Maiden tattoo on his right forearm that you can't miss. The tattoo looks a lot tougher than I am, I won't bite so if you know of some juicy Perl gossip, let me know it too so that I can tell the world.

If you are still having a hard time finding me, here is the itinerary that I am going to *try* to follow. I am the type who gets side-tracked easily so if something catches my eye, I'll probably wind up there instead.

See you at the conference!
