Perl Style: Avoid Gratuitous Backslashes

  • Perl lets you choose your own delimiters on quotes and patterns to avoid Leaning Toothpick Syndrome. Use them.
        m#^/usr/spool/m(ail|queue)#
        qq(Moms said, "That's all, $kid.")
        tr[a-z]
      [A-Z];
        s { /          }{::}gx;
    s { \.p(m|od)$ }{}x;
